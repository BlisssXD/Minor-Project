About the website

This website is used to search the Protein folding kinetics data and associated structural data for those proteins.


Protein Folding Kinetics

Predicting protein kinetic is crucial for comprehending the fundamentals of protein structure and energetics, and has application to diseases including Cystic Fibrosis, Type II Diabetes, Alzheimer's and others associated with protein misfolding. High Quality data is essential to develop precise models to predict and rationalize the behaviour of any system, including protein folding. One of the major obstacles for every researcher modelling a process is gaining access to a comprehensive, verified dataset. Learn more here.
